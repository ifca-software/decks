Why Malaysian GPUs Cost More

Malaysian providers charge a premium.
Here's why — with the numbers.

1. Tiny Supply vs Global Marketplace

SNS AI Factory: 64 H100s (32 commercial). Vast.ai: 17,000+ GPUs, 1,400+ providers, 500+ locations.

Sources: VulcanPost · Vast.ai

2. Electricity Costs Rising

MY tariff: 45.62 sen/kWh (~$0.105). US low-cost: $0.04–0.07/kWh. 40–60% cheaper.

Sources: DCD · Site Selection Group

3. No Price Competition

Only 3–4 MY providers. Vast.ai is a live auction with hundreds of hosts undercutting each other.

4. Enterprise Packaging

MY bundles ISO27017, PCI-DSS, SLAs, local support. Vast.ai gives you a bare VM.
