PMF Decision

Decision Framework

Declare PMF when…

  • Sean Ellis >40% "very disappointed"
  • Logo retention >95%
  • NRR >100%
  • Sales cycle <60 days
  • Unprompted referrals
  • Repeatable sales motion

If PMF not achieved…

  • Wrong market → pivot segment
  • Product gaps → prioritise features
  • Pricing mismatch → adjust model
  • GTM issue → change sales motion
  • Compliance issue → immediate fix
