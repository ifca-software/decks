The Product

Set up. Execute. Track.

FMX replaces WhatsApp groups and scattered vendor calls with one mobile-first platform — roll out across outlets and warehouses in weeks.

Set up

Register assets per outlet and warehouse — air-con, signage, forklifts, dock doors, cold rooms — and deploy standard PM schedules chain-wide. Prebuilt retail and warehouse templates included.

Execute

Mobile work orders for every task. Outlet managers and warehouse supervisors dispatch vendors directly. HQ sees every request, response, and closure in real time — safety compliance checklists built in.
