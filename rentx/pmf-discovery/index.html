PMF Decision

Decision Framework

Declare PMF when…

  • Sean Ellis >40% "very disappointed"
  • Logo retention >95%
  • NRR >120%
  • Sales cycle <100 days
  • FMX attach >40%
  • Repeatable sales motion

If PMF not achieved…

  • Wrong segment → focus on malls vs office
  • Product gaps → prioritise features
  • Pricing mismatch → adjust model
  • GTM issue → change sales motion
  • Low FMX attach → re-evaluate bundling
