The Problem

Budget hotels compete on price.
But the back office bleeds margin.

Four leaks that quietly drain economy hotel EBITDA.

  • 80–85% of bookings come through OTAs — at 15–20% commission, that's RM 15–20 lost on every RM 100 room

  • Housekeeping operates on paper checklists and walkie-talkies — rooms sit dirty while guests wait
  • Flat pricing year-round means missed revenue on peak nights and empty rooms on slow ones
